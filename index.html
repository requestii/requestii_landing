Who We Are

Requestii is a geolocation-powered freelancing platform designed to connect people with opportunities around them.

We believe that talent exists everywhere - but access to opportunity does not. Our mission is to bridge that gap.

The Problem

  • - Find nearby jobs quickly
  • - Access reliable service providers
  • - Trust online platforms that feel too global and disconnected

At the same time, skilled individuals remain unemployed or underpaid simply because they are not visible.

Our Solution

Requestii solves this by focusing on location and immediacy.

  • - Find jobs within your area
  • - Hire trusted people nearby
  • - Complete tasks faster and more efficiently

No complicated processes - just simple, local connections.
