About
1. Who We Are
Requestii is a geolocation-powered freelancing platform designed to connect people with opportunities around them. We believe that talent exists everywhere - but access to opportunity does not.

Our mission is to bridge that gap.

2. The Problem
In many communities, especially in developing regions, people struggle to:
- Find nearby jobs quickly
- Access reliable service providers
- Trust online platforms that feel too global and disconnected

At the same time, skilled individuals remain unemployed or underpaid simply because they are not visible.

3. Our Solution
Requestii solves this by focusing on location and immediacy.
We connect users based on where they are, making it easy to:
- Find jobs within your area
- Hire trusted people nearby
- Complete tasks faster and more efficiently

No complicated processes - just simple, local connections.

4. What Makes Us Different
- Location-Based Matching: Jobs and services are shown based on your real location
- Instant Connections: No long bidding processes - connect quickly
- Trust and Transparency: Built for real people in real communities
- Built for Emerging Markets: Designed with Africa and growing economies in mind

5. Our Vision
We envision a world where anyone, anywhere, can access opportunities around them with just a few taps.
Requestii aims to become the leading local opportunity network - empowering youth, freelancers, and small businesses to grow and thrive.

6. Our Mission
To make work accessible, local, and instant for everyone.
